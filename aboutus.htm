Our Philosophy
At Paul the Tutor's Education Center, we are committed to helping students develop core cognitive skills such as problem solving and critical reasoning. Our approach is based on Paul's Logical Learning System™, a method that uses academics as a launching pad to teach students how to read, write, and reason better. We believe that better thinking leads to better grades and better test scores, and that the skills we teach will help students succeed in academics and beyond.

Paul Osborne
Paul Osborne
Paul Osborne (Paul the Tutor) has been tutoring traditional and nontraditional learners for over 20 years. As a dyslexic learner himself, Paul developed an early interest in helping students who learn differently. He studied cognition at the Harvard Graduate School of Education and is the author of The LD SAT Study Guide, a manual specifically designed for students with learning disabilities. Paul is the creator of the Logical Learning System™ and MSL Math.
