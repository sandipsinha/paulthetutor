Distance Learning – How It Works

 

How does it work?
The student sees and hears instruction on their screen in as the tutor speaks and writes. We use a digital writing pad and special software – all YOU need is a computer with internet access. (If you have a webcamera, this allows two-way video, but it is not necessary). This arrangement lets you see what we WRITE – like notes, explanations, calculations – not merely what is typed.

For most people, this offers the only access to tutors with understanding of how to prepare students with learning disabilities.

To connect, we can use your favorite method of video chat: Skype, Facebook, Google Talk, or other service. To arrange a session, or to ask us a question, please contact us.
