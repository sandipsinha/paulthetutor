Cancellation Policy
 
  1. Cancellations made less than 2 days in advance are subject to a $10 fee.
  2. Cancellations made within 24 hours of an appointment are subject to a fee equal to 50% of the scheduled time.
  3. Rescheduling done within 24 hours of an appointment will be assessed a $10 fee.
  4. If a student misses an appointment without canceling, the full fee for the session will be due.
  5. House call appointment times are approximate, as we are at the whim of traffic as well as the needs of those clients with whom we meet prior to your appointment. We always do our best to arrive at the appointed start time, but might arrive late. We will do my best to call if we will be more than 15 minutes late.
  6. Anyone who terminates tutoring with Paul the Tutor's and hires their Paul the Tutor's tutor directly agrees to pay Paul the Tutor's a $1000 finders fee.
  7. If tutors miss an appointment, the parent will receive a 50% discount on the make up appointment.
  8. If you miss or cancel more than 2 sessions in a row, we may not be able to hold your spot on our calendar, and it may be given to another student.
  9. Deposits on classes are non-refundable.
  10. Once a class starts, no refunds will be given on class fee.
