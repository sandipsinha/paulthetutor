FAQ

What subjects do you tutor?
At Paul the Tutor's Education Center, we can match you with a tutor that specializes in your subject area. Our team is knowledgeable and diverse, with advanced backgrounds in the sciences, mathematics, and the humanities. Each of our tutors has completed training with Paul the Tutor, and understands how to help students develop core cognitive skills while improving grades and test scores.

What ages do you tutor?
Most Paul the Tutor's clients are in high school and junior high, but our tutors have experience with primary grades students, as well. Tutoring is also available for college and graduate students.

Do you provide specialized guidance for students with learning disabilities?
Yes. Paul the Tutor's is known for providing quality tutoring and test preparation to students with learning disabilities and alternative learners. Many of our clients who have had unsuccessful tutoring experiences in the past finally find the approach they need with Paul the Tutor's. Paul, who is himself dyslexic, understands the unique challenges faced by LD students, and has trained his team to be responsive to these challenges. Paul is also the author of the LD SAT Study Guide, the only SAT manual written for students with learning disabilities.

Do you offer tutoring for advanced students and conventional learners?
Yes. We work with students of all levels and abilities, including honors and advanced placement students. Our Test Preparation services for the SAT, ACT and SAT Subject Tests can help students score in the highest percentiles, gaining entrance into some of the most competitive colleges and universities.

Do you tutor on-site, or will you come to our home?
Paul the Tutor's Education Center is located on Piedmont Avenue in Oakland. Tutors often travel to students' homes for a small additional fee.
