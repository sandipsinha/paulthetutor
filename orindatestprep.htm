SAT Prep Classes at Orinda Academy

Course Description    |    Schedule

Beginning in 2011, Paul the Tutor's Education Center will offer SAT Prep classes at Orinda Academy. Classes will take place after school on the Orinda Academy campus and will help students to overcome challenges LD and alternative learners face on the SAT.

All classes are taught by Paul the Tutor's Instructors and are based on Paul's LD SAT Study Guide, the only SAT manual designed for students who learn differently. Instructors provide exposure to real SAT questions while teaching disability-specific strategies and techniques for students with both traditional and extended time. Students from other schools are welcome to attend.

LD SAT Prep at Orinda Academy includes:

  • Over 25 hours of prep time
  • Real SAT Practice questions
  • 2 proctored exams
  • Classes with a maximum of 10 students
