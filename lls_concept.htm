Logical Learning System™ - The Concept

Cognitive skills are the foundation of success

Cognitive Skills - one's ability in problem solving, understanding, memory, communication, and any other form of thinking.

1) Cognitive skills are far more useful than academic proficiency.
2) Cognitive skills can and should be developed using academics as a tool.
3) Focusing on cognitive skill development leads to greater academic performance.
Academics offers students the opportunity to develop and use cognitive abilities, it does not teach them to do so. Failing to recognize the difference can have devastating effects.
