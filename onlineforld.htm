Online Tutoring for Students with Learning Disabilities

 

Paul the Tutor's is known for providing quality tutoring to students with learning disabilities. Paul has tutored students who learn differently for over 20 years, and received an Ed.M. in Cognition from the Harvard Graduate School of Education. From dealing with dyslexia himself, Paul understands the unique challenges LD students face. He and his staff work closely with students to help them overcome these challenges and reach their academic and life goals.

The online tutoring program provides the multiple learning modes that help students take advantage of this expertise. With live, personal attention, audio and visual tutoring styles go beyond a text-driven approach to provide the help and support each student needs.
